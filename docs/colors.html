In this page you can find codes for colors, used to program in Compy.

Color Codes

These are the meaning of the codes used in commands like sc.printf() or sc.set_bg_color().

  • 0: light_blue
  • 1: blue
  • 2: light_green
  • 3: green
  • 4: pink
  • 5: red
  • 6: light_orange
  • 7: orange
  • 8: light_violet
  • 9: violet
  • 10: yellow
  • 11: brown
  • 12: white
  • 13: light_grey
  • 14: grey
  • 15: dark_grey
  • 16: black
  • 17: cyan
  • 18: purple
  • 19: deep_pink
